Process

Our group decided to hew close to the major theme of our class and trained our teachable machine to detect skin-tone from light to dark. We noticed right away that the model had difficulty, and mostly reported skin-tones as dark, despite robust data-sets and trouble-shooting. Referencing our data sets, we set up a frame using p5js to see if a particular region of the screen helped with detection, but this was unsuccessful. We each explore the reasons behind the difficulty with classification in our individual project statements. In initial group discussions we considered the possibility of algorithmic bias, and the hurdles presented by the "black-box" of the teachable-machines interface.
