Code, Power, & Machine Learning

Welcome to our LIS 500 Project 3 site! For this assignment, our group created a custom Teachable Machine image-classification model inspired by themes from Joy Buolamwini’s Unmasking AI.

Our goal was to explore how machine learning systems interpret visual data and how concepts like bias, representation, and power show up in these systems. We selected a subject related to the themes of the book, gathered images, trained a model through Teachable Machine, and then reflected individually on the implications of algorithmic decision-making.

Each of us has written a personal project statement, included on the “Lessons Learned” page, where we discuss what resonated with us from Unmasking AI and how the hands-on ML activity shaped our understanding of the relationship between technology, data, and social justice.
